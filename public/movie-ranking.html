The Best Movies According to Tiffani

My top 5 movies of all time.

Love Jones

Nia Long and Larenz Tate in the ultimate Black Love Story. #relationshipgoals for sure!

Usual Suspects

Quentin Tarantino at his best; a psychological thriller.

Girls Trip

This was a movie that mimics our infamous Miami trip in 2010.

The Hangover

The original, guys version of Girls Trip. Super Funny--I saw it in the theater twice!

Black Panther

Marvel. Black Superhero. Amazing to finally see a Superhero in the likeness of Black Excellence! #wakandaforever
